Welcome to EkoClub

The Elites of Lagos Welcome you!

Community Involvement

Our club is committed to giving back to our community. As a member, you will have the opportunity to participate in volunteer and philanthropic activities that support our local community in the US and Nigeria.

Networking Opportunities

Our club provides a platform for members to connect with other successful Nigerians living in Indiana. Our networking events are designed to facilitate meaningful connections and build long-lasting relationships that can benefit your personal and professional life.

Professional Involvement

Eko Elites Club offers members access to professional development opportunities such as workshops, seminars, and training sessions. These resources are designed to help you grow your skills and advance your career

Social Involvement

Eko Elites Club hosts a variety of social events throughout the year, including cultural festivals, family outings, and holiday parties. These events are a great way to meet new people and connect with other members of our community.

WorldWide Reach

Being an honorary member of Eko Elites Club does not confine us to just one location; we have branches of our club both in Nigeria and the diaspora, so you need never feel distanced from our great club!

About Us

Welcome to Eko Elites Club, a membership organization dedicated to bringing together successful indigenes of Nigeria living in Indiana, USA. Our club is focused on building a strong community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for networking, personal development, and giving back to our community.
